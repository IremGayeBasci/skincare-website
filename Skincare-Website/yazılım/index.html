THE IMPORTANCE OF SKIN CLEANSING IN SKIN CARE

Skin care is a routine that ensures that the skin stays well and supports skin integrity.
One of the most important points in the skin care routine is to be taken care of according to the skin type.

DAYTIME ROUTINE
1- Cleanse your skin from impurities with a water-based cleansing gel suitable for your skin type.
2- Apply tonic.
3- Apply a moisturizing cream suitable for your skin type.
4- Apply the appropriate sunscreen for your skin type.

NIGHT ROUTINE
1- Cleanse your skin from impurities and makeup with an oil-based cleansing gel.
2- Wash your skin with a water-based cleansing gel suitable for your skin type.
3- Apply tonic.
4- According to the needs of your skin, you can apply a serum suitable for your skin type.
5- Apply a moisturizing cream suitable for your skin type.

2- How do you feel your skin when you wake up in the morning?
A) He usually looks pale and dull.
B) I don't know, I didn't notice anything different.
C) My T-zone is usually oily.
D) I usually encounter a new pimple.

3- How do you usually feel after washing your face?
A) I stretch instantly and apply moisturizer first thing.

4- Which of the following is the problem that bothers you the most about your skin?
A) A constant feeling of tension
B) Wrinkles
C) Itching, flushing
